PIKI

PIKI is a Prague based project of four musicians. The story starts with the songs Petr wrote and decided to get them onto the stage. PIKI started around 2018 and the lineup was complete at the end of 2019.
Drummer Martin was soon joined by Olda on bass guitar in September 2019. Mirek joined the band in November as a guitarist. And that's it: Petr - singing guitarist, Martin on drums, Olda on bass guitar and Mirek on guitars.
The first gig took place in Prague club Potrvá in February 2020.
